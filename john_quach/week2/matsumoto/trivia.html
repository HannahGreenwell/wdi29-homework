His demeanor has brought about a motto in the Ruby community: "Matz is nice and so we are nice," commonly abbreviated as MINASWAN.
Matsumoto is married and has four children. He is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints,[11] did standard service as a missionary and is now a counselor in the bishopric in his church ward.[12]
Matsumoto received the 2011 Award for the Advancement of Free Software from the Free Software Foundation (FSF) at the 2012 LibrePlanet conference at the University of Massachusetts Boston in Boston.[10].
