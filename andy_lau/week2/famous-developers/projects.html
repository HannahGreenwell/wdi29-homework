  • In the Media

Jenn Schiffer (jennmoneydollars) is a web developer, an artist and a tech satirist in Jersey city. She is the director of community engineering at glitch. Her visual art has been described as "neon abstract pixel erotica". She mainly works in node and javascript with her web development works which are under the categories of creative coding and comedy.

  • Projects

Jenn's art focuses on pixel art both digitally and in real life. Her passion project has been a project which she started a few years ago called make8bitart. It's a free in-browser open web application. Over 1000 visitors daily use it and it's open source!
